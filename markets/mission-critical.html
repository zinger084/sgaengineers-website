Capabilities

What we design for mission-critical work

Full-discipline MEPFT with commissioning and construction administration, tuned to uptime, density, and service access.

  • Critical power distribution, UPS topology, and generator / ATS design
  • Redundancy planning (N, N+1, 2N thinking) with concurrent maintainability
  • Cooling strategies for high-density and high-reliability spaces
  • Thermal load analysis and growth planning for future IT load
  • Grounding, bonding, and electrical studies (including arc-flash support)
  • Fire protection including preaction and clean-agent approaches where appropriate
  • Data hall, MDF, and IDF design coordination with technology pathways
  • Structured cabling, cable management, and pathway capacity planning
  • BAS, BMS, and monitoring coordination for critical environments
  • Commissioning and functional testing of critical systems before go-live
  • Cutover-aware construction administration and field problem-solving
  • Peer review and criteria support when a second set of eyes protects uptime

How we work

A delivery mindset built for critical cutovers

Define reliability

Align on tier intent, maintenance windows, single points of failure, and what "online" must mean for your operators, not only the bid set.

Engineer topology

Power and cooling paths that can be built, tested, and serviced. Clearances, isolation, and sequencing are design decisions, not afterthoughts.

Coordinate early

BIM coordination, technology pathways, fire, and life-safety interfaces resolved before fabrication and rough-in lock you into bad options.

Prove and cut over

Commissioning, functional tests, and construction administration that protect live operations through startup and turnover.
