A full range of services - from traditional to niche.

SGA ENGINEERS has experience in all aspects of mechanical, electrical, plumbing, fire alarm/suppression, and technology engineering design. We deliver through design-bid-build, design-assist, CMR, design-build, and integrated lean project delivery.

How we work

Coordinated, precise, construction-aware

We are solutions-minded engineers. We help solve problems, not create more - using BIM and CAD for coordination, plus lidar and laser scanning, point clouds, 360° capture, drones, AR/VR, and AI where they make documents more accurate and construction less wasteful.

Partners also hire us because we are responsive. Working engineers stay on the project, answer directly, and keep RFIs and decisions moving. Principals stay close on every job. Integrity comes first in every scope, limitation, and recommendation, including green building principles and infection-control-aware design where the market demands it.

We are also often hired to lead the wider design team - coordinating architecture, structural, civil, interiors, and specialty consultants with our MEPFT package when the owner needs one design lead instead of a handoff maze.
