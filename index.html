Common questions

Before you send an RFP

Straight answers for teams comparing consulting engineers online.

Do you work with architects on design-bid-build and alternate delivery?
Yes. We deliver through design-bid-build, design-assist, CMR (Construction Manager at Risk), design-build, and integrated lean project delivery. Tell us your delivery model and we will structure engineering support around it.
Can one firm cover mechanical, electrical, plumbing, fire, and technology?
That is core to SGA. Full MEPFT plus commissioning means fewer handoffs between consultants and tighter coordination of power, HVAC, plumbing, suppression, and low-voltage systems.
What makes your designs more constructible?
Leadership and staff with hands-on installation experience. We design for clearances, sequencing, serviceability, and real equipment constraints, and use BIM, scanning, and field tools to reduce guesswork.
Do you support healthcare, industrial, and mission-critical work?
Yes, and we intentionally cross-pollinate lessons across markets. Redundancy thinking from mission-critical informs healthcare; process rigor from industrial strengthens education and commercial work. See our markets for the full list.
How do we start a conversation?
Email engineer@sgaengineers.com with project type, location, schedule, and any RFP or teaming notes, or use our contact form. We respond as working engineers, not a generic sales desk.

Evaluating engineers for your next pursuit?

Send the project brief, schedule, and teaming needs. We’ll tell you quickly how we can help, and what good looks like for your systems.
