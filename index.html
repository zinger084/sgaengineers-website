Common questions

Before you send an RFP

Straight answers for teams comparing consulting engineers online.

Do you work with architects on design-bid-build and alternate delivery?
Yes. We deliver through design-bid-build, design-assist, CMR (Construction Manager at Risk), design-build, and integrated lean project delivery. Tell us your delivery model and we will structure engineering support around it.
Can one firm cover mechanical, electrical, plumbing, fire, and technology?
That is core to SGA. Full MEPFT plus commissioning means fewer handoffs between consultants and tighter coordination of power, HVAC, plumbing, suppression, and low-voltage systems.
What makes your designs more constructible?
Leadership and staff with hands-on installation experience. We design for clearances, sequencing, serviceability, and real equipment constraints, and use BIM, scanning, and field tools to reduce guesswork. Coordinated documents and plain-language options help the whole team decide and build with fewer surprises.
How responsive are you once a project is moving?
We treat speed and availability as part of the engineering service: calls and emails answered by people who know the work, field questions handled quickly, and RFIs and site support that keep construction moving. We aim to respond within one business day on new inquiries, and faster when a live project needs it.
Do you support healthcare, industrial, and mission-critical work?
Yes. Lessons from one market help another: redundancy thinking from mission-critical informs healthcare, and process rigor from industrial strengthens education and commercial work. See our markets for the full list.
How do we start a conversation?
Use our contact form or email engineer@sgaengineers.com with project type, location, schedule, and any RFP notes. We aim to respond within one business day as working engineers - not a generic sales desk.
