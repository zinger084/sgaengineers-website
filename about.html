Why SGA

Experts with integrity. Quality on schedule.

SGA ENGINEERS is a full-service consulting engineering firm headquartered in Canton, Ohio. Founded by Jared F. Singer and Jason S. Geraghty, we deliver Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, Fire Protection, Technology, and Commissioning (MEPFT) for owners, architects, and contractors across Ohio and beyond. We are solutions-minded engineers: we help solve problems, not create more.

Our difference

Construction literacy is our advantage

Our teammates have direct, hands-on installation experience. We step beyond the engineering design realm and understand installation clearances, phasing, spooling, sequencing, serviceability, and project scheduling.

We don’t hand off projects after award. The teammate you originally meet is the teammate who sees it through - providing quality services throughout design and construction. Principals stay close on every project, large or small - leadership is not reserved for the biggest jobs.

We are also often the design team lead. When the project needs a single thread of responsibility, we coordinate architecture, structural, civil, interiors, and related partners alongside our MEPFT work so the full package stays aligned.

How we work

Mentor. Innovate. Lead.

Three principles from our firm profile that guide how we design, staff projects, and grow the next generation of engineers.

Mentor

We invest in co-ops, engineer interns, and rising designers. Principals stay close enough to teach through real projects - load calcs, field walks, submittals, and hard conversations - not just hand off markups.

Innovate

BIM coordination, lidar and point-cloud documentation, open BAS design, energy and carbon-aware thinking, and practical use of new tools where they improve accuracy and constructability - not technology for its own sake.

Lead

Integrity, collaboration, and technical excellence on every drawing set and site visit. We set a high bar so projects meet today’s needs and leave lasting value for owners and facility teams.
